Key Findings

Environmental Hotspots

The analysis identifies the following as the most significant contributors to environmental impact:

  1. Apple Cultivation (40.6%): Largest impact driver due to fertilizer use, irrigation, and agricultural machinery
  2. Production & Distillation (27.1%): Energy-intensive distillation and aging processes
  3. Packaging (20.3%): Glass bottle production represents significant embodied carbon

Recommendations

Immediate Opportunities (0-6 months)

  • Renewable Energy: Transition distillery to 100% renewable electricity (potential 15% reduction)
  • Lightweight Packaging: Reduce bottle weight by 10% (potential 2% overall reduction)
  • Local Sourcing: Increase percentage of local apple suppliers within 50km radius

Medium-term Initiatives (6-18 months)

  • Regenerative Agriculture: Partner with apple suppliers implementing carbon farming practices
  • Process Optimization: Implement heat recovery systems in distillation process
  • Alternative Packaging: Evaluate lower-impact packaging alternatives

Long-term Strategy (18+ months)

  • Carbon Neutrality: Develop pathway to carbon-neutral production by 2030
  • Circular Economy: Implement closed-loop packaging return system
  • Supply Chain Integration: Establish direct partnerships with sustainable apple growers
