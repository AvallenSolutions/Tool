Continuous Engagement Framework

We are committed to maintaining regular, meaningful dialogue with all our stakeholders. Our engagement framework includes:

  • Quarterly investor updates on sustainability performance
  • Annual customer surveys on sustainability preferences
  • Monthly employee feedback sessions on sustainability initiatives
  • Community advisory panels for major project decisions
  • Regular supplier engagement on sustainability requirements

Looking Forward

Our stakeholder-centric approach will continue to evolve as we deepen our understanding of stakeholder needs and expand our sustainability impact. Together, we are building a more sustainable future that creates shared value for all.
